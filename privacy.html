Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 21, 2026
The short version: Hearth is built so your data never leaves your device. Your recordings, transcripts, and notes are created and stored on your device. We never receive, store, or see any of it. There are no accounts, no servers, and no trackers in this app.

1. Who we are

Hearth is made by an independent developer ("we", "us"). Contact: asumathiapple@gmail.com.

2. Your data stays on your device

When you use Hearth, the following are created and kept only on your device, in the app's private storage:

  • Audio you record or import
  • Transcripts made from that audio
  • Notes, summaries, and chat created from that text
  • Client names and context you type
  • App settings (for example, your theme color)

None of this is uploaded, unless you yourself export or share it using system features. We have no copy and no way to access it.

3. What we collect about you

Nothing. Hearth has no server. It needs no account, email, or sign-in. It does not collect your contacts, location, or device identifiers. It contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking software. We do not sell or share personal information, because we have none.

4. How the AI works

Transcription uses Apple's on-device speech recognition. Notes, summaries, and chat use Apple's on-device intelligence (Apple Intelligence). These run locally on supported devices. Hearth does not send your audio, transcripts, or notes to us or to any third-party AI service.

5. Apple services

Hearth runs on Apple platforms and uses Apple-provided features: speech recognition, Apple Intelligence, iCloud (only if you back up), and the App Store for purchases. Your use of these is covered by Apple's Privacy Policy, not ours.

6. Optional iCloud backup

You can back up manually to your own iCloud Drive. The backup is stored in your iCloud account, protected by Apple, under your Apple ID. We cannot read, access, or delete it. Backup is off until you choose to run it.

7. Payments

Subscriptions are sold and processed by Apple through the App Store. We never receive your payment card or billing details. Purchases, renewals, and refunds are handled by Apple under your App Store account.

8. Crash reports

Hearth contains no analytics of its own. If you have turned on "Share with App Developers" in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements, Apple may give us aggregated, anonymous crash data. You control this in iOS settings. It never includes your recordings, transcripts, or notes.

9. Information about other people

Hearth is often used to record sessions with other people. Because everything stays on your device and we never receive it, you are the only controller of that information. You are responsible for getting any consent required by law and by your professional rules before recording anyone, and for following the confidentiality duties that apply to you.

10. Health and clinical use

Hearth's on-device design is intended to support your confidentiality. However, we are not a HIPAA "Business Associate", we do not sign Business Associate Agreements, and we make no promise that your use of Hearth complies with HIPAA, GDPR, or any other law. Meeting your legal and professional duties is your responsibility.

11. Deleting your data

You control everything. Delete a recording in the app to remove it and its notes from your device. Deleting the app removes its local data. Any iCloud backup you made stays in your iCloud until you delete it there. We hold none of your data, so there is nothing for us to delete for you.

12. Security

Your content stays inside the app's storage on your device, protected by iOS sandboxing and your device's own protections (passcode, Face ID, device encryption). Because your data does not travel to any server, the usual risks of cloud leaks do not apply to Hearth.

13. Children

Hearth is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age in your region).

14. Your privacy rights

Laws like the GDPR and CCPA give you rights over your personal data. We can honor these simply: we hold no personal data about you, and we do not sell data. Everything already lives on your device, fully under your control. For data held by Apple (for example, your purchase history or iCloud), contact Apple.

15. Changes

If the app's data practices ever change, we will update this policy and its date, and reflect the change in the app's App Store privacy details before that change ships.

16. Contact

Questions about privacy: asumathiapple@gmail.com

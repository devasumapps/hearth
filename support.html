Common questions

Where is my data stored? On your device. Recordings, transcripts, and notes never upload. If you choose to back up, the backup goes to your own iCloud account, which only you can access.
Which devices can write AI notes? Devices with Apple Intelligence: iPhone 15 Pro or newer, or an M-series iPad. Recording, transcription, playback, clients, and search work on all supported devices.
How does the free trial work? Your first 30 days include full access to everything. No card and no account needed. After 30 days, the free plan keeps unlimited recording, transcription, clients, search, and PDF export, plus a few AI notes each month.
How do I cancel my subscription? Open iPhone Settings → your name → Subscriptions → Hearth Pro → Cancel. Or in the app: Settings → Manage subscription. You keep access until the end of the paid period.
How do I restore my purchase? In the app: Settings → Restore purchases. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you bought with.
How do I move to a new phone? On the old phone: Settings → Back up to iCloud now. Wait for iCloud to sync. On the new phone, install Hearth, then Settings → Restore from iCloud.
How do I delete my data? Delete recordings inside the app, or delete the app itself. We hold no copy of your data, so there is nothing to delete on any server.
Do I need consent to record? Yes. You are responsible for getting consent from the people you record, as the law and your professional rules require where you practice.
